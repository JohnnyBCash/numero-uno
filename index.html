About Bitcoin Power Law

The Bitcoin Power Law model suggests that Bitcoin's price follows a power law relationship with time since its genesis block (January 3, 2009). This model has shown remarkable accuracy in tracking Bitcoin's long-term price trajectory.

The power law equation: Price = a × Days^β

Where β (beta) represents the slope of the trend on a logarithmic scale, and R² measures how well the model fits historical data.
